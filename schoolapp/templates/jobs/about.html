About

Febmex International Group of Schools is conceived to provide sound International education in an atmosphere of strong moral upbringing for young Nigerians. The focus is not only to enhance their psychomotor and effect attainments but to prepare them for self-reliance. Our philosophy of strong moral upbringing with religious values continue to be an essential aspect of our cardinal guild. with Outstanding teachers, and committed support staff are engagaged to actualize standout students and leaders for the country.

The school has well equiped Laboratories, I.C.T Centre, Basic Technology Workshop, Creative Art Studio, Music training, Language Class as well as good sporting facilities.

Vission:

To be recognised among the best schools in the world, with quality education and moral upbringing.

Mission:

Developing bright leaders for the Nation.
